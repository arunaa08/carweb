Audi is changing. Continuously. Discover here how our innovations ensure "Vorsprung durch Technik," how modern technology and progressive thinking characterize Audi, which processes are behind them and how our engineers work to further develop the existing.Audi is shaping the sustainable mobility of tomorrow, combining innovative technologies with progressive design along the way. Now is exactly the right moment for courageous and creative minds to ask unfamiliar questions and leave well-trodden paths.Autonomous driving, virtual reality or artificial intelligence: For Audi, the term mobility encompasses much more than just getting from point A to point B. In this way, Audi wants to help ensure that new technological possibilities can be used responsibly.

In 2026, Audi's home page serves as a definitive portal for the brand's transformation into a provider of sustainable premium mobility, deeply rooted in its core philosophy of "Vorsprung durch Technik". The brand's digital presence currently highlights a pivotal milestone in its history: as of 2026, Audi will only launch all-electric models onto the global market, with a commitment to gradually phasing out internal combustion engines entirely by 2033. This strategy, known as "Vorsprung 2030," is complemented by a vision of reaching company-wide net carbon neutrality by 2050, supported by the transformation of all global production sites to net carbon-neutral operations by 2025. Beyond electrification, the home page emphasizes a comprehensive digital ecosystem that integrates advanced software and connected services, such as the myAudi app, which offers over 100 functions for real-time vehicle management and on-demand upgrades.
