Up-to-Date Thanks to over-the-air

Sophia Schupritt works in Digital Lifecycle Management at Audi – an important area that optimizes the entire life cycle of a vehicle at regular intervals. With her expertise, she ensures that our vehicles are always up-to-date with software updates. The aim: To make the mobility of tomorrow even more comfortable and sustainable.

From intern to expert – that sounds exciting. Can you tell us more about your career at Audi?

Sophia: My professional development at Audi has shaped me both professionally and personally. It all started with an internship during my master’s degree, where I gained my first insights into the world of Audi. I then wrote my master’s thesis, also at Audi, where I deepened my specialist knowledge. I was then able to work at various locations and in different teams as part of the Audi Global Graduate Program (AGGP). This experience enabled me to build a strong network and continuously expand my knowledge. Each station has supported me in my development and confirmed my desire to work for Audi in the long term.
