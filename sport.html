Audi's global home page for 2026 centers on its "Vorsprung durch Technik" (Progress through Technology) philosophy, emphasizing a transformation into a leading provider of sustainable premium mobility. The current brand focus is heavily anchored in electrification and digitalization, with a commitment to launching only all-electric models globally from 2026 onwards. Key 2026 highlights featured on the site include the debut of the Audi Revolut F1 Team set for a January 20, 2026, launch in Berlin, and the introduction of "Audi Signature Care" which includes standard scheduled maintenance for all new models. The brand continues to prioritize a "360-degree" approach to sustainability, aiming for net carbon-neutral production sites and circular value chains.

In 2026, Audi's home page serves as a definitive portal for the brand's transformation into a provider of sustainable premium mobility, deeply rooted in its core philosophy of "Vorsprung durch Technik". The brand's digital presence currently highlights a pivotal milestone in its history: as of 2026, Audi will only launch all-electric models onto the global market, with a commitment to gradually phasing out internal combustion engines entirely by 2033. This strategy, known as "Vorsprung 2030," is complemented by a vision of reaching company-wide net carbon neutrality by 2050, supported by the transformation of all global production sites to net carbon-neutral operations by 2025.
