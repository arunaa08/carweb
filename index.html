About us

Audi is a German luxury automaker, part of the Volkswagen Group, known for sophisticated, performance-oriented vehicles featuring advanced tech like its famous quattro all-wheel drive and premium interiors, with models ranging from sedans (A4, A6) to SUVs (Q3, Q5) and electric options (e-tron), symbolized by its four interlocking rings representing its founding history. Heritage & Brand: Founded by August Horch, the four rings symbolize the 1932 merger of four auto companies (Audi, DKW, Horch, Wanderer). Its motto is Vorsprung durch Technik ("Advancement Through Technology"). Pioneers in all-wheel drive (AWD) with the Quattro system, offering superior traction and handling.
